WE love photography

Photography is a way to take pictures using a special camera. When you take a photo, you capture a moment in time. People use photography to remember special events like birthday parties or holidays. You can also take pictures of beautiful places like the beach or a forest. Cameras have a lens that lets in light, which helps create the picture. Some cameras can take colorful photos, while others are black and white. It’s fun to look at photos because they can show us things we might forget. Photographers can be anyone who loves taking pictures, from kids to grown-ups. Sometimes photographers even take pictures of animals or plants. When you take a photo, you can print it out and keep it in a photo album to look at later. Photography is like having a special memory that you can keep forever. So, next time you see something cool, grab a camera and snap a picture to save the moment!
